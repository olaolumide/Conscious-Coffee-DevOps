About Us

The first thing to know about us is that Concious Coffe is an organic Coffee company base in Norway with shops around the country.

Founded in 1980 in Oslo with the ambition of producing the best coffee using organic coffee bean. We want to reach-out to the whole country through our product. As a result, we establish Conscious Coffee shop in every part of the country. We have vision of reaching out to the world with the best organic coffee.

Conscious Coffee with her variety coffee drinks has mini pastry product to go along the cooffe drinks. Delievering the best coffee drink is our piority.
